The Plants We Are Growing

Basil

Basil is one of the most popular herbs used in cooking. Originating in India, it has a peppery and sweet flavor adding awseomness to almost any dish. It is also easy to grow from a seed.

Oregano

Oregano is native to countries in the Mediterranean and Western Asia area. This herb has a perfect balance of sweet and spicy. Oregano can enhance the flavor of almost any sauce. It is also a hardy plant which is easy to grow in pots.

Radishes

The Radish originated in china, gradually spreading west. Over time, raddishes became important foods of Greece, Rome, and especially in Egypt. This vegetable has a crisp, spicy (similar to garlic), and zesty taste.
